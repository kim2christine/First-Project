My First Violin

Chapter 4

After a few days, Doah was tired and needed to freshen up. Christine decided to give him the ultimate makeover! Here are some ideas for how you can spruce up your violin.
First things first. If the sound coming from your violin sounds dull, it might be a good idea to change the strings.
There are many brands to choose from
but the strings Christine decided to go with are Evah Pirazzi Gold Strings.
To change the strings, focus on removing AND replacing one string at a time.
Make sure not to remove all the strings simultaneously, because the other three strings need to have enough pressure to keep the bridge from collapsing
I like to start with the A string, then G string, then D string, and save the E string (the most fragile) for last.
And THAT is how Doah got his first little upgrade.
