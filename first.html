My First Violin

Chapter 1

This is so cool! that I am getting to share the story of how I got my violin!
His name is Doah- short for Shenandoah.
A long time ago, mommy Christine was looking for a new violin
and she came across a magical forest in the Shenandoah Valley.
There! She came across the most beautiful sound coming from a lone violin.
An invisible angel must have been playing it, for there was noone else around.
The violin came floating to her, like a wand picking a wizard,
and THAT is how Christine got her first violin.

Chapter 2

Christine and her new violin named Shenandoah decided to head back home to Alexandria
However, Christine realized she was running short on supplies.
In order to make it back, she made a list of the items she needed:

  • bread
  • cheese
  • milk
  • jam

To the store we go!

Chapter 3

After getting all of the items needed for the trip,
Christine made the trip home. As soon as she got home,
she got out her laptop and wanted to share with the world her new violin!
First she went to her mom, "Mom do you want to hear my new violin?"
Then, she went to her friends, "Do you want to hear my new violin?"

However, this method was not quick enough!
Christine wanted to give access to whoever needed encouragement
from the violin's magical powers.
Thus, she made a channel to share with the world!
